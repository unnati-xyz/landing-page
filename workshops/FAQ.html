FAQs



Should I bring my laptop?

Yes, we expect participants to bring their own laptops. We will send you a set of software packages that will be used in the workshop one week before the event.

Will there be internet at the venue?

Yes, we will provide internet access at the venue.
However, we advise all participants to be ready with the required environment setup for the workshop in advance and not depend on the internet at venue for the same.

What are the set of packages used during the workshop? How do we setup the required environment?

We will be sharing a detailed instructions of required packages and environment setup on the github repository one week before the workshop.

What are the timings of the workshop?

The workshop is a two day event. On both the days the venue will be open from 0800 hrs to 1730 hrs.

Are breakfast and lunch served as part of the workshop?

Breakfast, lunch and tea will be served at the workshop venue (at no extra cost) Generally we follow the below break timings
  • 0830 - 0930: Breakfast
  • 1115 - 1145: Tea Break
  • 1300 - 1400: Lunch
  • 1530 - 1600: Tea Break

I do not know python / R. Can I attend the workshop?

The introductory workshops are designed for beginner who have no background in programming. Simple programming concepts are used and are taught at the begining of the workshop

I'm bad at math / statistics and I'm scared of it. Can I attend the workshop?

We do not expect a prior math / statistics background. The concepts will be taught as part of the introductory workshop

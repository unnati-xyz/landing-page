WORKSHOPS @ UNNATI




Motivation

One of the core values of Unnati involves giving back to the community. The community played a major role in making us become the Data Scientists that people belive in and hence it is very close to our hearts.

As a company we concentrate on conducting paid workshops on the various aspects of Data Science. But as individuals, everyone at Unnati feels strongly about giving back to the community. Because of this, you'll find us hanging around in local and national meetup groups. We are the organizers of the Bangalore R Data Scientists group. We also conduct workshops and give talks at BangPypers - Bangalore's local Python user Group.

So when you attend a workshop conducted by Unnati, you can rest assured that we truly understand what the participants look forward to and expect. We aim to strike a balance between keeping our workshops very hands on and also teaching the theory and intuition that goes being making those decisions - Exactly what we expected when we attended workshops.

Data Science at Scale

The biggest problem in the field of Data Science is building platforms that scale. You'll find that running the same Machine Learning models or even simple Data Cleansing tends to fail when dealing with data that spans 100s of GBs. Its at this scale that things start to get more complex. This workshop is aimed at building Data pipelines that can handle large amounts of data using Apache Spark and performing analytics at scale.
